# SmartOps
### AI-powered customer follow-up for Nigerian food and fashion businesses

---

## The problem
A home cook in Lagos has 150 WhatsApp customers.
Every week, some go silent. She never knows who or why.
She loses N50,000–200,000 monthly to customers she
simply forgot to follow up with.

## The solution
SmartOps tracks every customer automatically and tells
the business owner exactly who to call today — before they lose them forever.

## How it works
1. Business owner adds customers (name, phone, last order)
2. SmartOps calculates how long each customer has been inactive
3. Every day it generates a "call list" ranked by urgency
4. Alerts are delivered via WhatsApp (coming Month 2)

## Built for
- Home cooks taking WhatsApp orders
- Caterers with repeat corporate clients
- Meal prep and frozen food delivery businesses
- Any Nigerian food or fashion business with loyal customers

## Tech stack
